What is the challenge?

Not a course. A design experience.

The IterateUX AI-UX Design Challenge is a mentor-guided learning experience where designers practice solving real-world UX problems in the age of AI.

Participants work on realistic product scenarios involving AI-assisted experiences — exploring human-AI interaction, trust, explainability, and ethical design — and produce a portfolio-ready case study demonstrating real design thinking and product strategy.

This is a conceptual challenge created for learning and portfolio development. It is not affiliated with any real company or client project.

The 6-week structure.

Each week has a focus, deliverables, and mentor checkpoints — so you always know what you're building toward.

Week 1

Kickoff, Foundations & Problem Alignment

Form your team and anchor your project in a meaningful problem space. Team formation, domain research, selecting a challenge prompt.

Defined problem statement Team working agreement Initial project hypotheses
Week 2

Research & AI Risk Mapping

Understand user needs and the ethical boundaries of your AI solution. User interviews, competitive analysis, identifying AI risks.

Research insights summary User pain point map AI trust & safety guidelines
Week 3

AI Opportunity & Framing

Identify exactly where AI adds value to the user journey. Journey mapping, defining the AI's role (assistant vs. automation), HMW statements.

AI Opportunity Map Behavior-focused personas High-level solution direction
Week 4

Concept & Interaction Flow

Translate your strategy into a tangible end-to-end user experience. Crazy 8s, AI interaction loops (Input → AI → Output), low-fi wireframing.

User scenario diagrams End-to-end interaction flows Low-fidelity wireframes
Week 5

Design, Trust & Iteration

Refine your solution into a high-fidelity, believable AI product. Mid-fi prototyping, designing for AI uncertainty/failure states, mentor feedback.

Clickable mid-fi prototype AI communication patterns Edge case & failure state designs
Week 6

Storytelling & Final Showcase

Package your process into a professional case study and present to the community. Case study structure, presentation coaching, live showcase.

Portfolio-ready UX + AI case study Professional presentation deck Certificate of completion

Frequently asked questions.

What is the AI-UX Design Challenge?
A 6-week, mentor-guided learning experience where designers solve real-world UX problems in the context of AI — producing a portfolio-ready case study showcasing design thinking, product strategy, and AI-aware UX skills.
Is this a real project or a conceptual challenge?
This is a conceptual challenge grounded in real-world product scenarios. You won't build actual AI models — you'll focus on designing thoughtful, ethical, user-centered experiences around AI systems, just like real product teams do. It is not affiliated with any real company or client.
Who is this best suited for?
UX & Product Designers (junior → senior), designers exploring AI in UX, career switchers entering UX, and senior designers looking to expand strategic and AI thinking.
What will I create by the end?
A complete, portfolio-ready UX case study including: problem definition & research insights, AI-aware product concept, user flows & interaction design, wireframes or prototypes, and design reasoning & storytelling.
Do I need prior AI or UX experience?
No AI experience required — the program teaches AI-aware UX concepts (trust, transparency, ethical design) from the ground up. Basic UX familiarity is helpful but mentorship and a structured framework guide you throughout.
How is this different from other design challenges?
Unlike typical challenges, this offers: a structured end-to-end UX process, mentor-guided feedback throughout, focus on AI + real-world scenarios, emphasis on design thinking & decision-making, and a portfolio-ready outcome — not just visuals.
How much time do I need each week?
Plan for 10–12 hours per week, depending on your pace and depth of exploration.
Will I receive mentorship?
Yes. You'll receive ongoing feedback from experienced UX mentors helping you improve design decisions, strengthen UX thinking, and refine your case study throughout the 6 weeks.
Will I work alone or in a team?
You'll work in a collaborative team environment, similar to real product teams — building skills in collaboration, communication, and presenting design decisions.
What tools will we use?
Typically Figma, Miro, and Notion — you're free to use tools you're comfortable with. Discord is the community communication platform.
Will I receive a certificate?
Yes — a certificate of completion, plus the opportunity for community recognition, LinkedIn shoutouts, and recommendations for top teams.
What is the cost?
$200 USD for the full 6-week experience, including mentorship, workshops, and the final showcase.
Do mentors receive compensation?
Yes — mentors receive a $130 honorarium per team as appreciation for their contributions.
How do I contact you with more questions?
Email design.challenge@iterateux.com or join the IterateUX Discord and leave a message in the #ai-ux-design-challenge channel. We answer fast.
