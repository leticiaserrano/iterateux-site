Questions, answered.

How much time will I need each week?
Plan for 15–20 hours per week — team meetings, client check-ins, research, design work, and presentations.
How will I be placed in a team?
A short questionnaire on your skills, availability, and interests. We match diverse teams to balance skills and experience. Team assignments are final — navigating collaboration is part of the real-world experience.
Do I need prior UX experience?
No — committed beginners are welcome. Basic familiarity with Figma and solid UX 101 knowledge will help you get the most from it.
What if I miss a workshop?
All workshops are recorded. Team meetings require live attendance, but your team can coordinate async work if something comes up.
How is this different from an internship?
More creative ownership, direct business interaction, and a shorter, more intense timeline — designed to grow you quickly and produce a polished case study.
