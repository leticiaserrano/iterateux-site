Flagship Program

Design the future of AI-powered experiences.

A 6-week, mentor-guided challenge where you work in a team on realistic AI product scenarios — human-AI interaction, trust, explainability, ethical design — and finish with a portfolio-ready UX + AI case study.

6 weeksRemoteTeam-based Mentor-guided10–12 hrs/week$200 USD No AI experience required
Week 1

Kickoff & problem alignment

Form your team, pick a challenge prompt, and anchor the project in a defined problem statement.

Week 2

Research & AI risk mapping

User interviews, competitive analysis, and mapping AI risks — bias, trust, and failure points.

Week 3

AI opportunity & framing

Journey mapping, defining the AI's role (assistant vs. automation), and HMW statements.

Week 4

Concept & interaction flow

Crazy 8s sketching, AI interaction loops (input → AI → output), and low-fi wireframes.

Week 5

Design, trust & iteration

Mid-fi prototyping, designing for AI uncertainty and failure states, mentor feedback rounds.

Week 6

Storytelling & final showcase

Case study structure, presentation coaching, and a live showcase to the community — plus your certificate.

A hiring manager's eyes on your portfolio — before the interview.

The Portfolio Review Sprint is a free, 2-week program of structured 1:1 reviews run by expert mentors with 4+ years in UX and product design.

  • Week 1: a real hiring-style review of 1–2 case studies — storytelling, UX decision-making, business impact, visual hierarchy.
  • Then you iterate on the feedback.
  • Week 2: a final 1:1 to validate improvements and get interview-ready guidance.
